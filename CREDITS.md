# Third-Party Asset Credits

## Tile artwork

The tile face SVGs in `MahjongmacOS/Assets.xcassets/Tiles/*` are from the
[FluffyStuff/riichi-mahjong-tiles](https://github.com/FluffyStuff/riichi-mahjong-tiles)
project, released under **CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain Dedication)**.

CC0 does not require attribution, but acknowledgement is good practice and
welcomed by the author.

### Asset inventory

- `Front.svg`, `Back.svg`, `Blank.svg`
- `Man1.svg`–`Man9.svg`, `Man5-Dora.svg`
- `Pin1.svg`–`Pin9.svg`, `Pin5-Dora.svg`
- `Sou1.svg`–`Sou9.svg`, `Sou5-Dora.svg`
- `Ton.svg` (East), `Nan.svg` (South), `Shaa.svg` (West), `Pei.svg` (North)
- `Haku.svg` (White), `Hatsu.svg` (Green), `Chun.svg` (Red)

Replacing the tile artwork is a drop-in: any SVG with the same filename will
take over. PNG @1x/2x/3x can replace SVG by editing the `.imageset/Contents.json`.
